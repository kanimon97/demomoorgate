How to use this demo
Step 1
Add your Public Key
Go to Settings and fill in your Vapi Public Key. This is different from the API key — find it in Vapi dashboard under API Keys labelled "Public".
Step 2
Click the microphone
Click the green orb above. Your browser will ask for microphone permission — click Allow. The agent connects within a few seconds.
Step 3
Speak naturally
Respond as a lead would. Try saying you need asset finance, or that you're busy, or ask to speak to someone now. Test every scenario.
Suggested test scenarios
Hot lead Say: "Yes I need asset finance for a new van, about £40,000, within the next two weeks"
Warm lead Say: "Maybe later in the year, we might need some working capital but nothing urgent right now"
Callback Say: "I'm in a meeting right now, can someone call me back Thursday afternoon?"
Not interested Say: "No thanks, we don't need any finance at the moment"
Has broker Say: "We already work with a broker actually"
